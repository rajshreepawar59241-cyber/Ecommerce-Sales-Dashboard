# Project Insights

Based on the dashboard analysis, the following insights were identified:

* Technology and Office Supplies were among the highest-performing product categories in terms of sales.
* A small number of products generated a significant share of the total revenue, indicating the importance of top-selling items.
* Sales showed seasonal fluctuations, with some months consistently outperforming others.
* Certain regions achieved higher sales but lower profit margins, highlighting opportunities to improve pricing and reduce costs.
* Customer purchasing behavior varied across regions, suggesting that region-specific marketing strategies could increase sales.
* Profitability depended not only on sales volume but also on discount levels and product mix.

# Final Conclusion

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive view of e-commerce sales performance through interactive visualizations and key performance indicators. It enables business users to monitor sales, profit, customer trends, and regional performance in real time.

The analysis identifies high-performing products and regions, highlights areas with lower profitability, and supports data-driven decision-making. Overall, the dashboard demonstrates how business intelligence tools such as Power BI can transform raw sales data into actionable insights that help improve revenue, optimize operations, and support strategic business growth.
